Microwavable Dinner

Fine dining has never been so cheap and convenient!

With a plethora of varieties to choose from, microwavable dinners are as easy to make as they are delicious!

Ingredients

  • A frozen dinner
  • A functional microwave

Steps

  1. Take the frozen meal out of the packaging
  2. Read the instructions on the back of the packaging for recommended heating time
  3. Place food in microwave
  4. When microwave timer has counted down to zero...take the food out.(Careful!It will be hot!)
  5. Enjoy!
