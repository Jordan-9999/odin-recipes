Bowl of Cereal

The Breakfast of Champions!

A bowl of cereal is a delicious meal no human on this earth should live without! And with such a simple convenient recipe, it's something even you can prepare!

Ingredients

  • A bowl
  • A box of your favorite cereal
  • Milk
  • A spoon

Steps

  1. Place the bowl in front of you.
  2. Pour the cereal into the bowl.
  3. Pour milk over the bowl of cereal.
  4. Grab your spoon.
  5. Enjoy!
