Toast

A crispy food that must be tasted to be believed!

Read on to find out how you can turn boring bread into something outstanding with just a push of a button

Ingredients

  • 2 pieces of sliced bread
  • A toaster
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Put the bread into the toaster
  2. Find the "On" button on the toaster
  3. Press the "On" button
  4. Wait a couple minutes
  5. When toast pops out, apply butter to toast
  6. Enjoy!
